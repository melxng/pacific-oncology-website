About Dr. Dong Xiang

Dr. Xiang is a board-certified medical oncologist and hematologist with over 15 years of experience in the field of oncology. He obtained his M.D. in 1989 from the Southern Medical University in Guangzhou, China and has M.S. degrees from the University of Alabama (1995) and the State University of New York (1997). He completed his residency in the New Hanover Regional Medical Center in Wilmington, North Carolina and his fellowship in the University of Louisville. He is dedicated to providing personalized and compassionate care to his patients and won the prestigious ASCO breast cancer merit award for his breast cancer vaccine research in 2011.

When he is not in the office, Dr. Xiang frequently enjoys hiking, fishing, running, table tennis, and spending time with his family. He has four daughters and enjoys vacationing and eating out at new restaurants with them.
